<objective>
Diagnose whether the SC-2 invariant_subspace_corr soft-fail (0.7833 pooled at n=12) is caused by the RNN encoding context-DM differently across modality_context ∈ {0, 1} and the pooled fit averaging across two distinct low-rank subspaces. Implements Gap 3 from 03-VERIFICATION.md (diagnostic, not a fix in itself).

Purpose: This plan does NOT directly attempt to lift corr above 0.85. Instead it answers "is the RNN actually two interleaved low-rank circuits, or is the structure pooled and the fitting just under-resolves it?". The answer informs 03-08's writeup commitment: if both per-context corrs are significantly above pooled (e.g. ≥0.85 each, or ≥pooled + 0.05), it's structural separation and Phase 3.1 closes with a story 1 reframe ("method/data alignment" rather than "method limit"). If both ≈ pooled, it's not the problem and Story 1/2 is unchanged from Gap 1's outcome.

Runs in parallel with Plan 03-05 (Gap 1) — independent inputs, no shared cluster artifacts. Cluster compute allowing.

Output: Two per-context Q.pt files; per_context_results.json with conclusion enum; SUMMARY.md.
</objective>
